Get PrEP Now

PrEP is Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis. This site focuses on PrEP for HIV prevention, which was first approved by the FDA in July 2012. There are two main sections - one for learning more about PrEP in general and the other for guidance on how to get on PrEP.

I developed this site while taking an online Web Design course at Metropolitan Community College - Penn Valley. The course content only focused on HTML and CSS.
