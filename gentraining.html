Genealogy Center Training
Project Site

For my final project while attending the Full Stack Web Developer training program at Centriq, I chose to design a mock Employee Learning Management System for the fictional Genealogy Training Center. I came up with the idea while doing genealogy research at the Midwest Genealogy Center in Independence, MO.

This project allows users to register with the app and then helps the user track their progress through the various courses and lessons provided on the site. Administrators can create, edit, and delete courses, lessons, and users.
